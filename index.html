What is PitchTeen?

PitchTeen is a tech startup based competition that is absolutely virtual and accessible to all teenagers around the globe. This event provides opportunities for participants to showcase their entrepreneurial skills.


What will participants be doing?

Teams of 1-4 will develop a startup idea and make their business plan.


Get Involved

Pitchteen 2021 Event:


In Summary

Who: high school students (ages 14-18 by event)

What: entrepreneurship & pitch competition

Where: online

When: March 14-21, 2021

Why: gain experience, win prizes, learn the startup process, work on business skills

Details

Prizes will be awarded in three categories. Check back later for more info.

1. Each participant: $120 in ShashiDo credits, chance for internship and Machine Learning Challenge with SashiDo.

2. 1st Prize (Overall): Framer 12 months PRO membership

3. Best Business Potential: Taskade PRO membership

4. Best UI Design: Balsamiq 90-day extended trial.

Questions & Answers

How do I sign up?

Applications are now open. Register here!

What if I don't have a team before the event?

It's okay if you don't have a team, we'll conduct enough sessions on zoom and slack and try to put you into existing teams.

How can I stay connected during a virtual event?

If you're looking to make connections outside your team, feel free to join our Slack community (link in footer).

Where will the competition take place?

The competition will completely virtual.

Who is eligibile to compete?

PitchTeen is open to all high school students, age 14-18.

How can I help organize the 2021 PitchTeen event?

We are accepting applications to operations, marketing, and tech positions. See above.

Is PitchTeen free?

Yes, PitchTeen is absolutely FREE!

Will there be prizes awarded to winners?

We are awarding prizes in three categories: 1st Prize, Best Business Potential, and Best UI Design. See above for details.

What do I need to participate?

Stable internet connection, a mic/webcam, and a team. We will find a team for you if you do not form one upon registration.
