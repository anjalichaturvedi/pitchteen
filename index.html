What is PitchTeen?

PitchTeen is a tech startup based competition that is absolutely virtual and accessible to all teenagers around the globe. This event provides opportunities for participants to showcase their entrepreneurial skills.


What will participants be doing?

Teams of 1-4 will develop a startup idea and learn how to develop their business plan.
