What is PitchTeen?

PitchTeen is a tech startup based competition that is absolutely virtual and accessible to all high school and college students around the globe. This event provides opportunities for participants to showcase their entrepreneurial skills.


What will participants be doing?

Teams of 1-4 will develop a startup idea and make their business plan.


Get Involved

Pitchteen 2021 Event:


In Summary

Who: high school & college students (ages 14-21 by date of the event)

What: entrepreneurship & pitch competition

Where: online

When: March 14-21, 2021

Why: gain experience, win prizes, learn the startup process, work on business skills

Details

Prizes & Awards

Prizes will be awarded in three categories. Check back later for more info.

1. Each participant: $120 in ShashiDo credits, chance for internship and entry to Machine Learning Challenge with SashiDo. Free 30 days course from Interview Cake. 3 Months' Hacker Plan from Repl.it

2. 1st Prize (Overall): Framer 12 months PRO membership

3. Best Business Potential: Taskade PRO membership

4. Best UI Design: Balsamiq 90-day extended trial.

5. Best High School Team: Magoosh SAT/ACT prep discounts.

6. Best College Team: 1 year subscription from 1Password.

Submission Guidelines

1. Each team must complete the submission form that'll be live during the event.

2. Each team must create a business plan explaining the problem statement, mission statement, intended audience, financial projections, and competitors' analysis.

3. A visual mockup must be submitted. It can be created through Wix, Framer, Figma, Adobe UX, other content management systems, illustrations, or it can take the form of a hand-coded website.

4. Your team may submit an optional video explaining the idea as well.

Judging Criteria

1. Creativity

2. Originality

3. Marketability (more details later)

Questions & Answers

How do I sign up?

Applications are now open. Register here!

What if I don't have a team before the event?

It's okay if you don't have a team, we'll conduct enough sessions on zoom and slack and try to put you into existing teams.

How can I stay connected during a virtual event?

If you're looking to make connections outside your team, feel free to join our Slack community (link in footer).

Where will the competition take place?

The competition will completely virtual.

Who is eligibile to compete?

PitchTeen is open to all high school & most college students, age 14-21.

How can I help organize the 2021 PitchTeen event?

We are accepting applications to operations, marketing, and tech positions. See above.

Is PitchTeen free?

Yes, PitchTeen is absolutely FREE!

Will there be prizes awarded to winners?

We are awarding prizes in five categories: 1st Prize, Best Business Potential, Best High School Team, Best College Team, and Best UI Design. See above for details.

What do I need to participate?

Stable internet connection, a mic/webcam, and a team. We will find a team for you if you do not form one upon registration.
